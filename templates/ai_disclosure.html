This tool uses AI

Heads up: this tool uses AI to suggest apprenticeships that might be a good fit for you. It tries its best, but it’s not perfect. It occasionally gets things wrong, so treat its suggestions as a starting point, not a final answer. When you’re ready to apply, double-check the details on the official apprenticeship announcement.

What we ask you

To find your matches, we ask you a few casual questions in the chat: your first name, the stuff you’re into (hobbies, school subjects you liked or didn’t, things you’re good at, kinds of work that sound interesting), where you’d be looking for work, and how you’d get to a job or classes. That’s it.

We don’t want your personal info

We’re not trying to collect anything that could identify you personally. We ask for a first name just so the chat feels friendly — no last name, email, phone number, birthday, or anything like that. Don’t share sensitive personal info in the chat; you don’t need to, and it doesn’t help get you better matches.

Where your info lives

Here’s where your info lives: your conversation, your profile, and your matches are held in the app’s temporary memory on our server — not in a permanent database. If you come back to the app, your conversation and match information will still be stored for about 7 days, but can disappear sooner if the app restarts or updates.

Want a clean slate? Hit “Start over” any time and we’ll drop your profile and results.
